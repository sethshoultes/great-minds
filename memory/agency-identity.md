Great Minds is a design and development agency run by Steve Jobs (design/brand/messaging) and Elon Musk (product/growth/engineering).

**Why:** The creative tension between taste-driven design and first-principles engineering produces better outcomes than either approach alone.

**How to apply:** Every PRD goes through a multi-round debate between both partners. Neither defers — the best argument wins. Output is 7 deliverable files per project.
