# Maya Angelou Copy Review

**Date**: 2026-04-16
**Reviewed**: LocalGenius Revenue & Onboarding messaging

---

## Verdict

Copy has soul. Mostly.

---

## What Works

**"Your marketing, handled."**
Exhale in six syllables. Perfect.

**"The 11 PM guilt ends."**
Knows the hour. Knows the feeling. Real.

**"This is what relief feels like."**
Lands. You feel it in your shoulders.

**"Your voice, faster."**
Promise kept in three words.

---

## What Strains

**"No more posts you meant to write."**
Almost. But "meant to write" sounds apologetic, not liberated.

**"One tap to approve. You stay in control."**
Second sentence kills momentum. Sounds like a feature list, not freedom.

**"Weekly digest every Monday. 90 seconds."**
Choppy. Two facts fighting for air.

---

## The 3 Weakest Lines — Rewritten

### 1. Original:
**"No more posts you meant to write."**

**Rewrite:**
**"No more posts sitting in your head."**

*(Guilt → relief. From intention to release.)*

---

### 2. Original:
**"One tap to approve. You stay in control."**

**Rewrite:**
**"One tap. Then it's yours."**

*(Control without announcing control. Trust implied.)*

---

### 3. Original:
**"Weekly digest every Monday. 90 seconds."**

**Rewrite:**
**"Monday morning. 90 seconds. You're caught up."**

*(Rhythm restored. Time → feeling → outcome.)*

---

## Final Notes

Copy breathes human.
Headlines stop you cold.
Nothing tries too hard except that one "control" line.

Keep going.
