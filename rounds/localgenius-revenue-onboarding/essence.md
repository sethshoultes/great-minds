# Essence

**What is this:** AI that handles restaurant marketing so owners don't have to think about it.

**Feeling:** Relief. The 11 PM guilt ends.

**What must be perfect:** Zero-interface dashboard. Shows what's done, not what to do.

**Creative direction:** Your marketing, handled.

---

## Synthesis

**Name:** Sous. One syllable. Right-hand chef. No explanation needed.

**Price:** $99/month. One tier. No games.

**Entry:** 14-day trial, credit card required. Filters tourists from customers.

**Core tension:** Speed vs. craft. Ship fast, but memorable. Both matter.

**Distribution wins:** Toast/Square partnerships. Case studies restaurant owners share. SEO for "how to get more reviews."

**Cut:** ROI calculators, freemium tiers, referral theater, three-tier pricing, FAQ sections.

**Keep:** Before/after metrics, Loom testimonials (upgrade video later), <1s load time, static page that ships this week.
