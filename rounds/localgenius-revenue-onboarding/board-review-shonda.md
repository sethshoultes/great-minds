# Board Review: LocalGenius Revenue & Onboarding
**Reviewer:** Shonda Rhimes
**Role:** Narrative & Retention Strategist
**Date:** 2026-04-16

---

## Story Arc: 4/10

**Signup → Aha gap is a cliff, not a bridge.**

- Landing: "11 PM guilt ends" — strong emotional hook
- Pricing page: Shows case study (Marcello's 90-day transformation)
- Trial signup: Dead zone. No mention of what happens after "Start trial"
- First 5 minutes: Ghost town. No onboarding narrative visible
- Aha moment: Undefined. When does relief actually arrive?

**Missing beats:**
- No "Day 1" promise
- No progression telegraphing ("Here's what happens next week")
- Case study shows 90-day arc, but trial is 14 days — narrative mismatch
- No emotional payoff structure

---

## Retention Hooks: 2/10

**Nothing brings them back.**

Tomorrow:
- No daily habit trigger
- No "review waiting" notification strategy
- No content queue preview

Next week:
- PRD mentions "Weekly digest every Monday" — but no detail on what makes it addictive
- No progression metric ("Your Marketing IQ is rising")
- No social proof loop ("3 restaurants like yours just got featured")

**What's missing:**
- Content calendar cliffhanger ("Next week's posts are wild")
- Performance comparison ("You're outpacing 80% of similar restaurants")
- AI learning feedback ("I just learned your voice better")
- Community elements (zero peer visibility)

---

## Content Strategy: 1/10

**No flywheel visible.**

Current state:
- AI writes posts, user approves — that's input/output, not a flywheel
- No user-generated content amplification
- No "best-performing posts" library shared across restaurants
- No seasonal content calendar hooks
- No local event auto-detection storytelling

**Missed flywheel opportunities:**
- High-performing review responses → template library → all users benefit
- Restaurant success stories → case study content → attracts more restaurants
- User approval patterns → AI voice tuning → better content → more approvals → better data
- Weekly digest → shareable to staff/investors → viral loop

**Data moat (Jensen's point) not being built:**
- Every restaurant teaches AI, but AI doesn't teach back across restaurants
- No aggregated "what's working" intelligence shared

---

- Trial ends → ... what? Do they lose all momentum?
- No "Day 13 preview" of what paid users get next

## What Needs to Ship Next

**If you want retention, add these:**

1. **Day 1 aha moment script:**
   - "Your first AI post goes live in 4 hours. Here's the preview."
   - "I analyzed your Google reviews. Here's what customers love most."

2. **Daily hook (bring them back tomorrow):**
   - Push notification: "Your Thursday post is ready to approve (15 sec)"
   - Email: "You have 1 review waiting + AI response drafted"

3. **Weekly cliffhanger (bring them back Monday):**
   - Sunday email: "Next week's content calendar is live. 3 posts about your spring menu."
   - "Monday Marketing Digest: Last week you earned $X in trackable revenue"

4. **Progression visibility:**
   - "Marketing IQ: 340 → 380 this week (AI learned your tone better)"
   - "Week 2 unlocked: Seasonal content suggestions now available"

5. **Peer curiosity:**
   - "Restaurants in Portland saw 40% more engagement using [strategy]. Try it?"
   - "Your review response rate beats 78% of similar restaurants"
