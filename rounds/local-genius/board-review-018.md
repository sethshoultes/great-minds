**Productize the agency itself.**

LocalGenius is not the destination. It is the proof of concept. You have now demonstrated that a multi-agent system — with real personas, real debate, real quality loops — can build production software from a PRD.

That process is worth more than any single product it produces.

The next move is not another client. It is packaging what you just did: the Great Minds workflow, the agent roster, the debate structure, the QA loop. Turn it into something repeatable. Something teachable. Something you can hand to the next team and have them produce the same quality.

The moat is not LocalGenius. The moat is the system that built LocalGenius.

Build that. Document it. Ship it.
