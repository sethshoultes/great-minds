# LocalGenius Sites: Consolidated Board Verdict

**Date:** April 14, 2026
**Reviewers:** Shonda Rhimes, Jensen Huang, Warren Buffett, Oprah Winfrey
**Project:** LocalGenius Sites — Phase 1 Deliverables

---

## Points of Agreement

All four board members converged on the following assessments:

### 1. Technical Execution is Strong
- **Unanimous praise** for Cloudflare infrastructure (D1, R2, Workers)
- Provisioning pipeline with circuit breakers is well-engineered
- Static generation in <5 seconds with 95+ PageSpeed scores
- Multi-tenant architecture with proper security isolation
- Cost structure is exceptional: ~$0.04/site/month infrastructure cost

### 2. The "Reveal Moment" is Compelling
- All reviewers recognized the emotional power of "website in 5 minutes"
- The transformation from "I need a website" to "that's MY website" is the product's core hook
- This is the right thesis for the target market

### 3. Post-Launch Retention is the Critical Gap
- **Shonda:** "No retention narrative, no content flywheel, no emotional cliffhangers"
- **Jensen:** "AI leverage for growth, intelligence, and defense is unexploited"
- **Buffett:** "No durable competitive advantage"
- **Oprah:** "The emotional architecture needs the same level of craft"

### 4. The Moat is Thin (Currently)
- Technology is replicable in 4-8 weeks
- No network effects
- Low switching costs (static HTML is portable)
- Data advantage not yet being captured or leveraged

### 5. Missing Pricing/CAC Validation
- No customer acquisition cost modeling
- No willingness-to-pay research conducted
- Viral coefficient assumptions unvalidated

## Overall Verdict

# PROCEED WITH CONDITIONS

The board unanimously supports moving forward, but with specific conditions that must be addressed before or immediately after launch.

### Rationale

1. **Infrastructure is production-ready** — The technical foundation exceeds requirements
2. **Market thesis is sound** — Targeting businesses with no web presence is a real opportunity
3. **Economics are attractive** — 99%+ gross margins at scale
4. **Core UX is compelling** — The reveal moment is emotionally powerful

### However

1. **Retention is not optional** — Without day-2 and day-7 reasons to return, churn will kill the business
2. **Pricing is unvalidated** — Assuming $79/month conversion without testing is a risk
3. **Moat requires active building** — Every month without data capture is a month competitors gain ground
4. **Emotional design is incomplete** — The user-facing journey needs the same craft as the backend

---

## Conditions for Proceeding

### Pre-Launch (Must Complete Before Day 0)

1. **Design the Reveal Moment Experience**
   - Map the user journey during 5-minute provisioning
   - Create celebration screen when site goes live
   - Film 5 real business owners seeing their sites; iterate until they gasp
   - *Owner: Product/Design*

2. **Implement Basic Traffic Notifications**
   - Day 1: "Your site is live!"
   - Day 2: "Someone visited your site today"
   - Day 7: "Here's how your first week went"
   - *Owner: Engineering*

3. **Add Human-Friendly Error Handling**
   - Replace technical messages with empathetic copy
   - "We're taking extra care with your site" not "Circuit breaker open"
   - *Owner: Product/Engineering*

### Week 1-2 Post-Launch (Must Complete by Day 14)

4. **Validate Pricing**
   - Put payment form in front of 100 potential customers
   - Test $79/month Pro tier + $149/month Managed tier
   - Document conversion rates and objections
   - *Owner: Product/Growth*

5. **Instrument Data Collection**
   - Log every structured field generated (menus, hours, services)
   - Begin building the data asset from Day 1
   - *Owner: Engineering*

6. **Define CAC Model**
   - Model the acquisition funnel
   - Determine if referral viral coefficient supports growth targets
   - Plan paid acquisition budget if needed
   - *Owner: Growth/Finance*

### Month 1 (Must Complete by Day 30)

7. **Build the First Week Email Sequence**
   - Day 1: Site live celebration
   - Day 3: "Here's what people see when they search for you"
   - Day 7: "Your AI has a suggestion"
   - *Owner: Product/Marketing*

8. **Film MCP Success Story**
   - Document one business owner using natural language updates
   - Publish as proof of AI-managed value
   - *Owner: Marketing*

9. **Resolve Naming**
   - Commit to "LocalGenius Sites" or "Presence"
   - Update all materials consistently
   - *Owner: Brand/Product*

### Month 2-3 (Critical for Retention)

10. **Launch Review Flywheel**
    - Automatic Google review ingestion
    - AI curation of testimonials
    - Notification when new reviews appear on site
    - *Owner: Engineering*

11. **Add Dashboard Heartbeat**
    - Simple analytics: visits, search appearances, call clicks
    - Not to compete with Squarespace, but to create reasons to return
    - *Owner: Product/Engineering*

12. **Implement 30-Day Site Refresh**
    - "Your AI has ideas for your site" milestone event
    - Creates anticipation and engagement
    - *Owner: Product/Engineering*

## Final Statement

LocalGenius Sites has built the stage. The infrastructure is world-class. The economics are compelling. The market is real.

But a stage without a story is just empty space.

**The board approves proceeding with the explicit understanding that retention mechanics, emotional design, and pricing validation are not post-launch "nice-to-haves" — they are conditions for continued investment.**

Ship the product. But ship it with the first-week experience designed, the pricing validated within 14 days, and the retention flywheel spinning within 30 days.

The difference between a $50M product and a $500M platform is not the technology.

It's the story you tell — and the story you help your customers live.
