## Idea 1: **WP Insta-Site** — WordPress Site from Screenshot

**The Problem:** 28 million WordPress developers. Every client meeting starts with "I want something like THIS" *shows screenshot*. Then weeks of back-and-forth translating vision to reality.

**The Solution:** Upload a screenshot or URL of any website. AI analyzes layout, colors, typography, sections. Generates a complete WordPress theme + content structure. Client sees their vision in minutes, not weeks.

**Market Size:** 810M+ websites run WordPress. Every agency, every freelancer, every DIY business owner.
**Technical Feasibility:** Vision API for analysis, structured output to theme.json + block patterns. We've done hybrid AI before.

**Target User:** WordPress agencies, freelancers, site builders
**Distribution:** WordPress.org, ProductHunt, agency newsletters
**Session Scope:** Screenshot upload, Claude vision analysis, theme.json generation, block pattern output

## Idea 2: **LocalGenius Lite** — One-Click Local Business AI

**The Problem:** LocalGenius is powerful but requires setup. 32 million small businesses in the US alone just want "make AI answer my customers."

**The Solution:** WordPress plugin. Install, enter your business type and location, done. Instantly generates FAQ content, adds a chatbot trained on your site, handles common queries. Zero configuration.

**Market Size:** Every local business with a WordPress site. Plumbers, dentists, restaurants, salons. Massive TAM, zero technical sophistication required.
**Technical Feasibility:** We literally built LocalGenius. This is a simplified, plugin-ified version.

**Target User:** Small business owners, WordPress site maintainers
**Distribution:** WordPress.org, local business forums, agency referrals
**Session Scope:** Plugin scaffold, auto-content generation, embedded chat widget, Cloudflare Workers AI backend

## Idea 3: **Video Press Release** — AI News Anchor for Any Announcement

**The Problem:** Companies write press releases nobody reads. Video gets 10x engagement but costs $5K+ to produce.

**The Solution:** Paste your press release or announcement text. Select an AI anchor style (professional, casual, tech). Get a broadcast-quality video with TTS narration, lower thirds, b-roll suggestions, and branded intro/outro. Export for LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter.

**Market Size:** Every company that announces anything. Product launches, funding, hires, features. Millions of announcements per day globally.
**Technical Feasibility:** We already have Remotion + TTS infrastructure. This is a productized workflow.

**Target User:** Marketing teams, founders, PR agencies
**Distribution:** ProductHunt, LinkedIn organic, AppSumo
**Session Scope:** Text input, TTS generation, Remotion template, export pipeline
