## What Was Built

LocalGenius Sites was the original WordPress multisite deployment for the LocalGenius platform. The project has been archived as the platform consolidated into the main LocalGenius monorepo architecture. This ship formalizes the archive status and documents the transition.

The consolidation ship validates the agency's decision to archive repositories that have been superseded by architectural improvements. Moving from a separate sites repo to integrated deployment reduces operational complexity and maintenance overhead.
