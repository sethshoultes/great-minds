**Shipped**: 2026-04-14

## What Was Built

This project established the emdash-intake-template — a standardized intake workflow template for the Great Minds Agency. The template provides a reusable structure for capturing project requirements, stakeholder information, and success criteria during the intake phase of new client engagements.

The template follows agency conventions for documentation, ensuring consistency across all intake processes and enabling efficient handoffs between phases of the GSD pipeline.
