What if you could hand a requirements doc to a room full of geniuses and wake up to a deployed website?

They don't agree with each other. That's the point. Debate produces better architecture than consensus ever will.

Every task burns tokens from a transparent credit balance. Scope creep doesn't sneak in — it asks permission.

You went to sleep. They didn't. By morning, your site is live.
