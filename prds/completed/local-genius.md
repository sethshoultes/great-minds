# PRD: LocalGenius

## Overview
LocalGenius is an AI-powered digital presence platform for small local businesses (restaurants, salons, plumbers, dentists, etc.). One conversational interface replaces the 6-8 disconnected tools most small businesses juggle — website builder, social media scheduler, review manager, email marketing, SEO, analytics, booking, and local ads. The owner talks to LocalGenius like they'd talk to a marketing employee, and it handles the rest.

## Problem Statement
Small local business owners are drowning in digital tools they don't understand and can't afford to hire someone to manage. They need a website, Google Business profile, social media presence, review responses, email campaigns, and local SEO — but they're experts at their craft, not marketing. The average local business owner spends 5-10 hours/week on digital marketing tasks they hate, or ignores them entirely and loses customers to competitors who don't.

Current solutions either:
- Cost too much (agencies charge $1,500-5,000/month)
- Require too much expertise (Mailchimp, Hootsuite, WordPress)
- Solve only one piece (Yelp, Square, Wix)

## Target Market
**Primary**: Solo operators and small local businesses with 1-20 employees, $100K-$5M revenue. Think: the dentist who's great at dentistry but posts on Instagram once a quarter.

**Secondary**: Franchise operators managing 5-50 locations who need consistent brand presence across all of them.

**Market size**: ~33M small businesses in the US. ~60% have inadequate digital presence. TAM ~$20B/year if capturing even a fraction of agency/tool spend.

## Core Features (v1)
- **Conversational command center**: "Post something about our lunch special today" → generates copy, image, posts to Instagram/Facebook/Google
- **AI website builder**: Generates and maintains a conversion-optimized site from a 5-minute onboarding conversation
- **Review autopilot**: Monitors Google/Yelp reviews, drafts responses, alerts owner for negative ones
- **Email/SMS campaigns**: "Send a reminder to customers who haven't visited in 30 days" → done
- **Local SEO agent**: Continuously optimizes Google Business Profile, citations, and local keywords
- **Weekly digest**: Plain-English report: "You got 12 new reviews (4.6 avg), 340 website visits, 23 bookings. Here's what I recommend this week."

## Success Metrics
- 10,000 paying users within 12 months of launch
- $29/month base tier, $79/month pro tier → $3.5M-$9.5M ARR target
- NPS > 50 (these users are used to hating their tools)
- <5 min/day average owner time spent in app
- 30% reduction in customer churn vs. comparable SaaS tools

## Constraints
- Must work for non-technical users (if it requires a tutorial, we failed)
- Must integrate with existing platforms (Google, Meta, Yelp, Square, etc.) — not replace them
- AI costs must stay under 15% of revenue per user
- GDPR/CCPA compliant for customer data handling
- Mobile-first — these owners live on their phones

## Competitive Landscape
- **GoDaddy/Wix/Squarespace**: Website-only. No AI orchestration across channels.
- **Vendasta/Podium/Birdeye**: Enterprise-priced. Complex. Built for agencies, not owners.
- **ChatGPT/Claude**: Can generate content but can't post, manage, or track anything.
- **Jasper/Copy.ai**: Content generation only. No execution layer.

**Our edge**: We're not a content generator OR a tool suite — we're an AI employee that actually does the work end-to-end.
